Biography

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, popularly known as Babasaheb Ambedkar, was an Indian jurist, economist, social reformer, and politician who inspired the Dalit Buddhist movement and campaigned against social discrimination towards untouchables. He was independent India’s first Minister of Law and Justice, and the principal architect of the Constitution of India.

Ambedkar was a visionary leader who worked tirelessly for equality, education, and social justice. His contributions have had a lasting impact on Indian society and continue to inspire generations.
